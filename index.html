Your Guide at the Table

A narrator, not just a host.

Every session is led live by a professional game narrator — someone who reads the room, escalates the stakes, and turns a rulebook into a plot. Rules fade into the background. What's left is tension, alliance, and the particular thrill of a table that's telling a story together.

  • Full rules teaching — arrive knowing nothing, leave fluent
  • Dynamic pacing tuned to your table's energy
  • In-character narration for immersive titles

The Atmosphere

One club, no fixed address.

We don't have a home base — we have a city. Every session finds a new corner of Istanbul worth gathering in.

☕

Cozy cafés

Tucked into a candlelit table in Cihangir or Kadıköy, where the coffee refills as often as the dice roll.

🌊

Seaside parks

A blanket, a board, and the Bosphorus breeze — some of our best campaigns have played out with a sea view.

🌆

Open-air gatherings

Rooftop terraces, courtyard tables, pop-up nights — if the city has a good corner, we'll find a reason to play there.

Whoever you are, whatever you've played before — you belong at this table. We welcome complete beginners and seasoned strategists alike, and every session opens with a warm, no-pressure rules teach. Come solo, come with friends; you'll leave with both.
